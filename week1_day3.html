Healthcare.gov Launch (2013)

Issue: Website crashes, security concerns, poor user experience

Lessons: Importance of testing, user-centered design, scalable architecture

Berlin Airport System (2008-2020)

Issue: 12-year delay, ‚Ç¨7 billion over budget

Issue: $170 million project scrapped

Lessons: Changing requirements, lack of user involvement, technical complexity

üéØ Key Success Factors

  • Clear Requirements: Well-defined, stable, and user-validated requirements
  • Strong Leadership: Executive support and skilled project management
  • User Involvement: Continuous stakeholder engagement and feedback
  • Realistic Planning: Achievable timelines and budgets with contingencies
  • Quality Assurance: Comprehensive testing and validation processes
  • Change Management: Proper procedures for handling scope and requirement changes

Pine Valley Furniture Case Analysis (25 minutes)

üè¢ Pine Valley Furniture Company

Background: Traditional furniture manufacturer founded in 1985, expanding into e-commerce and modern retail channels.

Current Challenge: Outdated inventory management system causing:

  • Stock discrepancies between stores and warehouses
  • Delayed customer orders and delivery issues
  • Inability to track real-time inventory levels
  • Poor integration with new online sales platform

Analysis Questions:

  1. What are the primary business problems to solve?
  2. Who are the key stakeholders?
  3. What are the requirements for a new system?
  4. What SDLC approach would you recommend and why?
  5. What are the major risks and how would you mitigate them?

Team Formation Workshop (30 minutes)

ü§ù Building Effective Project Teams

Team Composition Guidelines:

  • Size: 4-5 members optimal for collaboration and accountability
  • Skills Mix: Diverse technical, analytical, and communication skills
  • Complementary Strengths: Different perspectives and problem-solving approaches
  • Shared Commitment: Similar availability and dedication levels

Team Formation Process:

  1. Skill Assessment: Complete individual skills inventory
  2. Speed Networking: 5-minute conversations with potential teammates
  3. Team Assembly: Form teams based on complementary skills
  4. Charter Development: Create preliminary team charter
